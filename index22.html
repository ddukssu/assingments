Brant — Captain of the Troupe of Fools

About Brant

Brant is a 5-star Fusion Natural Resonator in Wuthering Waves.
From Rinascita, he leads the Troupe of Fools with charisma and flair.
Beneath his carefree mask lies a protector caring for his crew’s safety.
His versatile combat includes damage, buffs, healing, and shielding.
Debuted in Version 2.1 banner, he’s already a fan favorite.

“Every stage is a sea,
and I steer my own ship.”

  1. Introduced in drip-marketing (Version 2.0) as a sword user from Rinascita.
  2. Officially debuted in Version 2.1 banner with Phoebe and Changli.
  3. Rerun banner: March 15–28, 2025 (Asia) with Unflickering Valor.
  4. In-game role: captain of the Troupe of Fools, blending showmanship with leadership.
  • Fusion Resonator with Sword weapon type.
  • Versatile DPS with buffs, healing, and shielding.
  • 'Forte Circuit' system for powerful skill releases.
  • Leadership devoted to crew safety and freedom.
